How We Work

1. Place Your Order

Have a chat to one of our friendly staff members. They will point you in the right direction and they will be you point of contact throughout.

They will help you choose the right product with the right artwork option. They will also answer any questions you might have.

2. We Collobrate Together On Design

You can use any one of our online colloboration tools to share your artwork with us when placing your order.

OR

You can come in for assistance with editing your existing design or with the creation of a new one. We will help with creating the right design for your purposes.

3. We Print

Once artworks have been approved, we then move them into production where the final product will be made to your specifications.

It generally takes 48 hours to produce your product after artwork approval.

4. We Deliver or You Pick Up

Once you product is finished, we can ship all around Zimbawe from Harare to your selected destination (NB. Shipping may attract an extra cost).

You can also choose to come and collect from our work facilities.
